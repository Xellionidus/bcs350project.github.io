WEBSITE FROM SCRATCH PROJECT  
Pawfect Path Dog Training Website  

PART 1: CONTENT  

1. What is the name of your business?  
The name of the business is Pawfect Path Dog Training.

2. Is the business real or fictional?  
The business is fictional, but it is designed to resemble a real professional dog training service.

3. What products or services does the business offer?  
Pawfect Path Dog Training offers several dog training services including:

• Puppy foundation training  
• Basic obedience training  
• Behavior correction  
• Private one on one training sessions  
• Leash walking training  
• Owner guidance and dog behavior education  

These services are designed to help dog owners build better communication and control with their pets.

4. Who is the target audience?  
The target audience includes:

• First-time dog owners  
• Families with puppies  
• Owners of large breeds such as German Shepherds  
• Dog owners experiencing behavior problems  
• People looking for professional dog training support  
• People who cats get kidnapped  

5. What problem does this business solve?  
Many dog owners struggle with behavior issues such as barking, leash pulling, jumping, or lack of obedience. Pawfect Path Dog Training helps solve these problems by providing structured training techniques and guidance that improve the dog's behavior and strengthen the relationship between the dog and owner.

6. What makes this business unique compared to competitors?  
This business focuses on personalized training plans and positive reinforcement techniques. Instead of a one size fits all program, the training adapts to the individual dog's needs and the owner’s lifestyle. The business also emphasizes building trust and communication between the owner and the dog.

7. What pages will your website include?  
The website includes the following sections:

• Home  
• About  
• Services  
• Training  
• Testimonials  
• FAQ  
• Contact  

The navigation menu allows users to jump to each section of the website.

8. Write a short mission statement for the business.  
Our mission is to help dogs and their owners build trust, confidence, and better habits through positive and personalized training.

9. What tone should the website content have?  
The tone of the website is:

• Professional  
• Friendly  
• Informative  
• Supportive  

This tone helps make visitors feel comfortable while still showing expertise in dog training.

10. What call to action do you want visitors to take?  
The main call to action is:

Book a Free Consultation

Other calls to actions include:

• View Services  
• Contact Us  
• Watch Video Proof of Our Work  

11. What information must be immediately visible on the homepage?  
The homepage must clearly display:

• The business name  
• A clear headline describing the service  
• A short description of the business  
• A hero image of a trained dog  
• Navigation menu  
• A call to action button  

This helps users immediately understand the purpose of the website.

12. Will the site include images, videos, or other media? Why?  
Yes, the site includes images and a video link.

The images include German Shepherd training photos to match the dog training theme. Media elements help make the site more visually engaging and allow users to quickly understand what the business does.

A video link labeled “Watch Video Proof of Our Work” is also included to demonstrate training results.

PART 2: DESIGN  

1. What overall style will the website have?  
The website uses a clean design style. The layout is organized into sections and easy to navigate.

2. What color scheme will you use and why?  
The color scheme includes:

• Dark navy blue  
• White  
• Beige background  
• Green accent buttons  

These colors were chosen because they look professional, calm, and easy to read. The green buttons help highlight important actions such as booking a consultation also I just like the colors.

3. What fonts will you use for headings and body text?  
The site uses simple sans serif fonts such as Arial. These fonts are easy to read and work well across different devices and screen sizes.

4. How will spacing and layout improve readability?  
Spacing improves readability by separating each section clearly. Padding and margins prevent the page from looking crowded. Text is broken into short paragraphs, and service descriptions are displayed in card layouts to make them easier to read.

6. What visual hierarchy will guide the user’s attention?  
The visual hierarchy follows this order:

• Hero headline  
• Hero image  
• Call-to-action buttons  
• Services section  
• Training media section  
• Testimonials  
• FAQ  
• Contact form  

This structure guides the user from learning about the business to contacting it.

7. How will consistency be maintained across pages?  
Consistency is maintained through:

• The same color scheme across the entire site  
• Repeated button styles  
• The same fonts for headings and body text  
• Similar card layouts for sections  
• Consistent spacing and alignment  

8. What icons will you use?  
Simple icons such as paw prints, arrows, and check marks can be used to visually represent services and benefits.

9. What emotions should the design evoke in users?  
The design should make users feel:

• Comfortable  
• Confident  
• Supported  
• Motivated to train their dog  

10. How will accessibility be considered?  
Accessibility will be considered through:

• High contrast between text and background  
• Clear readable font sizes  
• Simple navigation  
• Mobile-friendly layout  
• Buttons that are easy to click  

11. What inspiration sources influenced your design?  
The design was inspired by modern service business websites and pet training websites. Clean layouts and simple navigation were used to make the website easy to use.

PART 3: INTERACTIVITY  

1. What interactive elements will your site include?  
The site includes several interactive elements including:

• Navigation menu  
• Buttons  
• Contact form  
• FAQ accordion  
• Testimonial slider  
• Video link button  

2. How will users navigate the website?  
Users navigate the site through the navigation bar at the top of the page. Clicking a menu item scrolls to that section of the page.

3. Will there be form validation or user feedback? Explain.  
Yes. The contact form validates user input to ensure required fields are filled in correctly. If the form is completed properly, a success message appears.

4. What JavaScript functionality will you implement?  
JavaScript is used to implement:

• Mobile navigation menu toggle  
• FAQ expand and collapse feature  
• Testimonial slider  
• Contact form validation  
• Success messages for form submission  

5. How does interactivity improve user experience?  
Interactivity makes the website feel more responsive and engaging. It allows users to interact with the content, navigate easily, and receive feedback when completing actions such as submitting a form.
